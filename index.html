Caribbean School of Theology (CST) is a mobile modular seminary, which offers advanced studies for Christian leaders, pastors, missionaries and those involved in teaching and administrating training programs. A board of directors, representing the participating Caribbean countries and an administrative committee, governs CST, which is guided by its constitution and official curriculum.
